Changelog for 0.2

Welcome to Classic 7 0.2! This is the next major release of Classic 7. Thank you all for the support for the previous builds! Anyway's let's get into it.

Here's what's new:

  • Switched from DWMBlurGlass to OpenGlass
  • Fixed Windows Media Center
  • Made the tray icons more accurate
  • Themes are now in the right order
  • Changed string from "Open Settings" to "Open Control Panel"
  • Theme colours are 100% accurate
  • Control Panel is almost 1:1
  • Add Ease of Access stuff such as: On-Screen Keyboard, Narrator and Magnifier
  • Fixed battery flyout
  • Moved Homegroup up in Explorer
  • Bunch of small other updates
